About Shoplens

We're building the future of AI-powered shopping — making it easy for everyone to find, compare and buy anything they see.

🚀 Our Mission

Shoplens was built with one goal — to make shopping smarter. We use cutting-edge AI vision to help people instantly identify products, compare prices across stores, and make confident buying decisions without getting scammed.

🤖 The Technology

We combine computer vision AI with real-time price comparison engines and scam detection algorithms. Our models are trained on millions of products to give you the most accurate results in seconds.

🔒 Privacy First

Your screenshots and searches are never stored or sold. We process everything in real-time and delete it immediately. Your privacy is our top priority — always.

👥 Our Team

Built by a passionate team of designers, engineers and AI researchers who believe shopping should be simple, smart and safe.
